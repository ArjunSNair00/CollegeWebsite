About Us

College of Engineering Karunagappally (CEK), established in 1999, is affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University and approved by AICTE. CEK offers quality education in engineering disciplines and is managed by the Institute of Human Resources Development (IHRD), a government undertaking.

Departments

  • Computer Science & Engineering
  • Electronics & Communication Engineering
  • Electrical & Electronics Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering

Admissions

Admissions are conducted through the Kerala Engineering Entrance Examination (KEAM). Candidates are selected based on merit. For more details, visit the official CEE Kerala website.
